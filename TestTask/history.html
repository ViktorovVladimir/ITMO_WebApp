History of cars

The beginning of the automobile industry

The first car was created at the end of the 19th century.

Exactly 100 years ago, in 1919, an interesting experiment was conducted at one of Henry Ford's automobile plants.

He decided to pay the crew of workers for rest, and not for work.

This was a team of repairmen whose job it was to keep the famous conveyor running smoothly.
